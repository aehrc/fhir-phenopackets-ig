1.0 StructureDefinition-Individual

Mapping Notes

This element is represented in FHIR as a profile of Patient.

Phenopackets FHIR Comments
id Patient.identifier  
alternate_ids Patient.identifier FHIR supports having multiple identifiers and specifying their type.
date_of_birth Patient.birthDate  
age N/A This attribute represents “an age object describing the age of the individual at the time of collection of biospecimens or phenotypic observations reported in the current Phenopacket”. This is problematic because it complicates representing longitudinal information. Therefore, in this IG, this attribute has been moved to Encounter-Phenopackets.
sex Patient.gender  
karyotypic_sex Extension Bound to the karyotypic sex valueset with strength of required.
taxonomy Extension Bound to the taxonomy valueset with strength of required.

1.0.1 Formal Views of Profile Content

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://ga4gh.org/fhir/phenopackets/StructureDefinition/Individual

The subject of the Phenopacket is represented by an Individual element. This element intends to represent an individual human or other organism.

This profile builds on Patient.

This profile was published on Fri Oct 18 20:32:59 AEST 2019 as a draft by Global Alliance for Genomics and Health.
