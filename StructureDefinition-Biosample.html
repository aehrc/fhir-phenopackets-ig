1.0.1 Formal Views of Profile Content

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://ga4gh.org/fhir/phenopackets/StructureDefinition/Biosample

A unit of biological material from which the substrate molecules (e.g. genomic DNA, RNA, proteins) for molecular analyses (e.g. sequencing, array hybridisation, mass-spectrometry) are extracted.

This profile builds on Specimen.

This profile was published on Mon Dec 02 08:48:39 AEST 2019 as a draft by Global Alliance for Genomics and Health.
